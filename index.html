Welcome to Howard County STEM!

With the increasing stress of finding the perfect major or the perfect job, high school can be difficult. The Howard County Mentorship site is a tool for Howard County students to gain access to contacts in various fields in order to ease the process towards university, jobs, and the future. Oftentimes it can be difficult to determine a path for the future, and site allows students to ask questions and learn more about the variety of career paths the world has to offer. This website is meant to give students the opportunity to find a mentor who can guide them along their way through schooling and the job search.

Our Mission:

Even with many strong opportunities in Howard County, many students are unsure of what's available. Hoco STEM aims to assist students in finding these opportunities to help develop a passion for STEM.

Our Resources:

Calendar: Our calendar displays a wide variety of events, such as hack-a-thons, conferences, and more! Be sure to check out the calendar regularly, as we update our calendar regularly.

Mentors: Interested in a particular field but not quite sure how to get started? It can seem daunting to pursue a career path when you have no guidance from a mentor, but our blog posts feature a wide variety of STEM professionals pursuing a wealth of careers.
